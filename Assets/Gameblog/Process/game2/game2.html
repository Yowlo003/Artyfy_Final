GAME BLOG

Hitman: Absolution is a 2012 stealth video game developed by IO Interactive and published by Square Enix's European branch. It is the fifth installment in the Hitman series

This game is a stealth game, you play as an assassin sneak in any way you want kill the target in any way you want may it be poisoning or strangle to death or just shoot them this game is completely challenging. I have enjoyed while playing this game I hope I can play more games like this.

I'll rate this game a 10 / 10 from the gameplay, Music, Sound design, Art and Story. This game is one of the games you may enjoy yeah its hard at first but when taste your first mission complete you well be more eager to play another mission your gonna be excited on what will happed next. Again its difficult but its rewarding, just wait until you unclock all the weapon and upgrades then you well be unstoppable. I recommend this game to anyone who want to try stealth game.
