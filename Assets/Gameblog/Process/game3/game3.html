GAME BLOG

Hades is a 2020 roguelike action role-playing game developed and published by Supergiant Games. It was released for macOS, Nintendo Switch, and Windows following an early access release in December 2018.

This game is a rouge-lite games that each time you where killed you well go back to the starting point. Many player found this frustrating but I enjoy it very much. Each run is completely different, also each time you die you well learn new abilities and grow stronger.

Overall I well rate this game a 10 / 10 from the gameplay, Music, Sound design, Art and Story. This is one of the games that you cant get bored while playing full of action pack gameplay one mistake of your dash or actions can lead your character to death. The characters enemies and there attack pattern is each to learn on but difficult to execute. Also that ending keep my edge to play more im excited to hades 2.
